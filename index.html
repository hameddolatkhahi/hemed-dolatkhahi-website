About Me

London, UK

Currently finalizing my Professional Diploma at the Digital Learning Institute, I combine 9 years of L&D leadership with hands-on digital development to bridge the gap between business goals and learner needs.

My background in Engineering fuels how I approach learning: I think in systems, prototype rapidly, and obsess over whether a digital solution actually transforms behavior. This technical foundation allows me to bridge the gap between complex subject matter and engaging learner experiences.

Most recently, as Head of L&D at Rahnema College, I orchestrated the end-to-end lifecycle (TNA to Evaluation) for 24+ annual bootcamps and a community of 2,000+ learners. Beyond strategy, I remained deeply involved in the "craft"—designing curricula and multimedia assets that align human growth with business KPIs.

Now based in London and completing my Professional Diploma with the Digital Learning Institute, I am focusing on high-impact Instructional Design and eLearning Development. This portfolio showcases my current work in creating scenario-based modules, interactive prototypes (Genially/Storyline), and micro-learning assets that solve real-world performance gaps.

Evidence-Based Design

Utilizing ADDIE & SAM to bridge performance gaps. I ensure every interactive element and content piece maps directly to a measurable learning objective.

Technical & Authoring Fluency

My engineering background enables me to master eAuthoring tools rapidly and collaborate seamlessly with LMS admins and technical SMEs.

Impact & Iteration

From NPS to behavioral metrics, I analyze learner data to evaluate effectiveness and continuously iterate for better learning ROI.

Modern Learning Standards

Committed to WCAG Accessibility and evidence-based design. I build high-quality, inclusive learning experiences that meet the rigorous professional standards of the UK's academic and corporate sectors.
